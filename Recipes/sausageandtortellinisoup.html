Sausage and Tortellini Soup

Description

This creamy soup is a play on a Italian Zuppa Toscana. Full of crumpled sausage, tortellini, and kale this soup makes the perfect winter meal. Serve with warm bread and salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound mild Italian sausage
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 70-ounces chicken broth
  • 1 20-ounce package cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flake, or to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium to large frying pan, add sausage. Cook over medium-high until browned. Once cooked set aside.
  2. Add olive oil to large pot or Dutch oven on medium-high.
  3. Add diced yellow onion to pot. Saute 5 minutes or until soft and golden. Add garlic and saute an additional 2 minutes
  4. Increase heat to high, pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste. Add the tortellini and continue to boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Reduce heat to medium, Stir in the heavy cream, cooked sausage, and kale. Let simmer for 5 minutes, or until the kale leaves have softened.
  6. Ladle soup into bowls and enjoy!
